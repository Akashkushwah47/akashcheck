Source transformation


What

Source transformation is a method to convert Voltage source to equivalent current source and Current Source to equivalent voltage source.


Why

Source transformation method are used for Circuit simplification.


How

We can convert voltage source to current source and vise versa without changing the characteristics of network that means voltage across a and b and current through network remains same after the transformation.


Voltage to Current transformation:

To convert voltage source to current source. We connect equivalent resistance R with parallel to current source and the value of current source: I=V/R;


Current to voltage transformation:

To convert current source to voltage source. We connect equivalent resistance R with series to voltage source and the value of voltage source: V=I/R
