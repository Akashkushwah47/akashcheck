We have a three method to find the direction of magnetic field :-
1. Maxwell's cork screw rule.
2. Right hand palm rule.
3.Right hand thumb rule.

Maxwell's cork screw rule

Suppose a screw is placed in the direction of conductor current flow. Now screw is rotated in such a manner that it moves in the direction of current flow . then the direction of magnetic field is in the same direction of rotation of the screw.

Right hand plam rule

If we open our right palm and keep our thumb and fingures perpendicular like that thumb will show the direction of current then normal to the palm will show the direction of magnetic field.

Right hand thumb rule

According to this rule if the current in the direction of stretched thumb then the direction of magnetic field will be in the direction of folding fingers of right hand.
