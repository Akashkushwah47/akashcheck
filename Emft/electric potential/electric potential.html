Electric Potential


     Electric potential is an electric field is defined as the amount of work to be done to bring a unit positive test charge form infinity to that point.

Suppose we have a point A in an electric field and q0 is a positive test charge then the electric potential at a point A will be

If work is done againt electric field( againt repultion or By external force) to bring test charge from infinity to that point then potential will be positive.

If work is electric field (attraction or by internal force) to bring test charge from infinity to that point then potential will be negative.

The earth potential is taken as Zero
It is taken as the reference potential to measure the potential of any body.
The electric potential above the earth potential is taken as positivde potential and electric potential below the earth potential is taken as negative potential.
